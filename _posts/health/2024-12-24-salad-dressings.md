# 1. Classic Lemon Vinaigrette
- **Ingredients**:
    - 3 tbsp olive oil
    - 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
    - 1 tsp Dijon mustard
    - Salt and pepper to taste
- **Instructions**:
    - Whisk all ingredients together until emulsified.
- **Salad Pairing**:
    - Mixed greens, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and grilled tofu/paneer.

# 2. Creamy Avocado Dressing
- **Ingredients**:
    - 1 ripe avocado
    - 3 tbsp Greek yogurt (unsweetened)
    - 2 tbsp lime juice
    - 1 clove garlic, minced
    - Salt and pepper to taste
- **Instructions**:
    - Blend all ingredients until smooth.
- **Salad Pairing**:
    - Romaine lettuce, corn, black beans, and diced red bell peppers.

# 3. Herby Olive Oil Dressing
- **Ingredients**:
    - 4 tbsp olive oil
    - 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
    - 1 tsp dried oregano
    - 1 tsp garlic powder
    - Salt and pepper to taste
- **Instructions**:
    - Mix ingredients in a jar and shake well.
- **Salad Pairing**:
    - Baby spinach, olives, feta cheese, and red onion slices.

# 4. Tahini Lemon Dressing
- **Ingredients**:
    - 2 tbsp tahini
    - 2 tbsp lemon juice
    - 2 tbsp water
    - 1 tsp cumin powder
    - Salt to taste
- **Instructions**:
    - Whisk ingredients until creamy.
- **Salad Pairing**:
    - Kale, roasted chickpeas, shredded carrots, and sesame seeds.

# 5. Apple Cider Vinegar Dressing
- **Ingredients**:
    - 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
    - 4 tbsp olive oil
    - 1 tsp Dijon mustard
    - Pinch of salt and pepper
- **Instructions**:
    - Shake ingredients in a jar until mixed.
- **Salad Pairing**:
    - Mixed greens, shredded cabbage, radishes, avocado, and scrambled eggs.

# Best Dressings for Organic Spring Mix

Organic spring mix, a tender blend of baby greens like arugula, spinach, and lettuces, pairs best with light and flavorful dressings that enhance its delicate taste. Here are some sugar-free dressing ideas:

---

## 1. Lemon Garlic Vinaigrette
- **Ingredients**:
    - 3 tbsp olive oil
    - 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
    - 1 small garlic clove, minced
    - Salt and pepper to taste
- **Instructions**:
    - Whisk all ingredients together until emulsified.
- **Why It Works**:
    - The tangy and garlicky flavors balance the mild and slightly peppery greens.

---

## 2. Simple Olive Oil and Balsamic Dressing
- **Ingredients**:
    - 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
    - 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
    - Pinch of salt and freshly cracked black pepper
- **Instructions**:
    - Shake the ingredients in a jar until well combined.
- **Why It Works**:
    - This classic pairing brings out the natural sweetness of the greens.

---

## 3. Avocado Lime Dressing
- **Ingredients**:
    - 1 ripe avocado
    - 2 tbsp lime juice
    - 2 tbsp water (to thin)
    - Salt and pepper to taste
- **Instructions**:
    - Blend the avocado, lime juice, and water until smooth. Add salt and pepper to taste.
- **Why It Works**:
    - Creamy avocado complements the tender greens while adding a healthy fat boost.

---

## 4. Herbed Yogurt Dressing
- **Ingredients**:
    - 3 tbsp plain Greek yogurt (unsweetened)
    - 1 tbsp olive oil
    - 1 tbsp lemon juice
    - 1 tsp fresh dill or parsley, chopped
    - Salt and pepper to taste
- **Instructions**:
    - Whisk all ingredients together until creamy and smooth.
- **Why It Works**:
    - Adds a refreshing, creamy texture without overpowering the greens.

---

## 5. Tahini Lemon Dressing
- **Ingredients**:
    - 2 tbsp tahini
    - 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
    - 2 tbsp water
    - Pinch of cumin powder
    - Salt to taste
- **Instructions**:
    - Mix the tahini, lemon juice, and water until creamy. Add cumin and salt to taste.
- **Why It Works**:
    - The nutty and tangy flavors enhance the slight bitterness of some greens in the mix.
